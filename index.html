Name Description and Explanation Type
Balloon They are an air type of troop where they only target defenses that attack other troops.
When they die, they drop a bomb that deals damage to nearby buildings,
clan castle troops, heroes, etc. When troops are on top of the balloon when the
balloon is taking down a defense, they get damaged. When all defenses are destroyed,
the balloon attacks any building now including the town hall.		 Elixir Troop
Wizard They attack anything thats close to them like walls, buildings, heroes, etc.
They provide support when attacking like barbarian king and wizards combined,
they make a great combo. They do splash damage, so when heroes or clan castle troops
and they come across the fireball splash damage, they get damaged. Wizards are aware
that when there are heroes or clan castle troops going near them, they attack them,
so that they don't die. They Are Ground troops.		 Elixir Troop
Lava Hound This troop only targets defenses and an air type troop, and when all defenses are destroyed,
they target any building now. They have high health and when they die, they release tiny lava pups
that attacks any type of building near them. These lava pups are weak, but fast.
The Lava Hound is a very good tank at the start to distract any defenses.
After making the Lava Hound attack and distract defenses, the Archer Queen or Balloon can be placed
behind the Lava hound so that they can destroy the defenses if timed right. 		Dark Elixir Troop
Barbarian King A strong hero that can be also a good tank with high health and decent damage. When at your base,
he defends your base when it gets attacked by others. During an attack of a player's base,
he will have a melee that is short ranged and targets any building near him. He cannot attack air troops.		 Hero
Archer Queen A ranged hero who deals good damage to buildings and walls. She provides a good range of
5 tiles away from her target. When at base, the Archer Queen defends the base and can attack ground
and air troops within the 5 tiles attack range. During attacks, she can be a good support and attacker by
just being at the back of tanks or troops and attack any building and walls so that the troops can go in much faster.		 Hero
Rage Rage provides troops and heroes more speed and more damage when inside the radius. Elixir Spell
Freeze Freeze can freeze buildings, heroes, and clan castle troops. And when they get frozen from the spell radius,
they can't move and attack until the spell timer runs out. It doesn't freeze your troops and heroes.		 Elixir Spell
Haste Haste provides troops and heroes a lot more speed than rage spell. No damage boost. Dark Elixir Spell
